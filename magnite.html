Nissan Magnite

The Nissan Magnite is a subcompact crossover SUV manufactured by Nissan. Unveiled in October 2020, the Magnite is placed below the kicks in Nissan's global SUV lineup, making it the smallest Nissan Crossover SUV worldwide. Manufactured in Chennai, India, it uses the same platform as the Renault Kiger that was introduced later and occupies the sub-4 metre crossover SUV segment in India. The vehicle is exported to several emerging markets worldwid, including Indonesia and South Africa.
