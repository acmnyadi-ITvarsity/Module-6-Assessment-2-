Nissan X-Trail

The Nissan X-Trail is a compact crossover SUV produced by the Japanese automaker Nissan since 2000. It was one of Nissan's first crossover SUV and released about the same time as several other companies' competing car-based crossovers. At its intoduction, the X-Trail is positioned below the truck-based Xterra and Pathfinder.
