Audi A3 Sedan

The compact-sized A3 lineup is the gateway car to the Audi brand, with two models available for 2020, the A3 40 TFSI FWD with front-wheel drive and the A3 45 TSFI Quattro with Quattro all-wheel drive. The former is available in Komfort and Progressiv trims while the latter adds the topline Technik trim.
All A3 models for 2020 now come standard with 18-inch wheels with all-season tires, Audi Advanced Key, front and rear parking sensors, anti-theft alarm with interior motion sensor and first aid kit. The base model FWD sedan is powered by a 184-horsepower, 2.0-litre turbo four-cylinder, Quattro models getting a more powerful 222-hp version of the same engine. All models are hooked up to a seven-speed S tronic transmission.
