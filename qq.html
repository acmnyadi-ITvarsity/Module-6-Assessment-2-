Nissan QashQai

The Nissan QashQai is a compact crosssover SUV produced by the Japanese car manufacturer Nissan since 2006. The first generation of the vehicle was sold under the nae Nissan Dualis in Japan and Australia and QashQai in other markets. The second generation, which was released in 2014, is not sold in Japan and is badged as the QashQai everywhere it is sold, except in the United States, where it is rebadged as the Nissan Rogue Sport.
