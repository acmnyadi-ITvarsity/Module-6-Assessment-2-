Infiniti Q50

The Q50 sport sedan, unchanged for the 2021 model year, is available in a choice of 300-hp or 400-hp 3.0L twin-turbocharged V6 engines, each mated to a seven-speed automatic with manual shift mode and downshift rev matching. It is offered in Pure, Luxe, Essential Tech, Sport Tech and two special edition models: Red Sport I-Line and Red Sport I-Line ProActive. Each of the six trims offers more of their own look and character. The Drive Mode Selector allows drivers to choose from five distinct modes, Standard, Snow, Eco, Sport and Sport+ (exclusive to the Red Sport I-Line) or customize to their own motoring style. In addition to the more powerful V6, the I-Line Red Sport come equipped with unique 19-inch sport wheels, a blacked-out double-arch sport grille, carbon-fibre rear decklid spoiler, black exterior mirror caps and more. The ProActive adds direct adaptive steering, lane departure prevention, active lane control, blind spot intervention and adaptive front lighting system.
